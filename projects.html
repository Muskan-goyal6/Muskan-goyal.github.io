Image Captioning Bot

May 2019 - Oct 2019

Use a CNN+LSTM based model architecture to generate captions that aptly describe images. Image captioning can have following applications:

  • Self driving cars : caption the scene around the car
  • Aid to the blind : guide them travelling on the roads
  • CCTV cameras : raise alarm against malicious activities
  • Google Image Search
